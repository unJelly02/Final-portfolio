Guzi's Makeover

Well, we didn’t want to be stuck on being another wishlist wishing for someone to check out our website! We wanted to have an instant connection and be remembered. So we decided to get sassy with our homepage.

We tested with two different versions of the homepage to understand our users. Testing these homepages gave us a lot of mixed responses as our older audience felt using a "bad word" was unnecessary to grab attention. Our younger audience however showed more positive responses as it was uncommon and wanted to read on.

Due to a deadline, we couldn't finish testing so we stick to using "Fork" for now until our tests responded otherwise.
