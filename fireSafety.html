Keep Kids Fire Safety Foundation
Redesigning the non-profit organization to teach fire safety to kids.

Story Behind

This is a redesign of a non-profit organization's website for our UI Design project. I participated in research,SWOT Analysis, wireframes of home and resources mainPage and UI design of Resources page.


THE PROBLEM

K-12 Educators need an engaging and interactive way to educate young children. How can we design a fun platform that gives a strong message surrounding fire safety?

ROLE

"Creating Wireframes and redesigning the resources page."

THE SOLUTION

Redesign the entire platform to capture more audience members. Make the website more engaging, easier to navigate, and that provides the resources parents and teachers need to integrate a fire safety program.
